Airport Ground Operations

Ensure the safety and comfort of all passengers who travel through the airport. Assisting passengers by answering questions, providing directions, or attending to their other needs. This 12 month course by INDO COCKPIT gives students the added impetus they need to excel in a career as a ground service staff for major domestic and international airlines.
Airport Ground staff are the first people who take care of their passengers in the initial stage. They must ensure the passenger's safety and comfort by issuing tickets and taking care of their luggage at the Airport till entering into Flight. The main duties of Ground staff are welcoming their passengers, issuing tickets, Boarding Pass, managing their luggage, and assisting disabled passengers & children. The Ground staff is always multitaskers, who should have excellent behavioral skills to provide excellent customer service inside the airport terminal.




Eligibility Criteria

  • Qualification: Minimum +2 or Graduate with good communication skills. Normal eyesight.
  • Age: 18-25 Years
  • Course Duration:1 Year & 6 Months

Ground Staff Jobs & Promotion Opportunity

  • Salary Range: 12k to 25k for fresher. Gradually increase to 2.5L for SR..Managers Based on Domestic/ international Airlines and Ground handling Companies
  • Job Titles: Customer Service Asst - Senior Customer Service Officer - Supervisor -Airline Manager - Airport Manager - Regional Airport Manager - General Manager.


Ground Handling has three categories, Customer Service, Ramp Service & Security Executive. Ground staff is assigned to these duties based on their skills. Ground staff responsibility starts from check-in to sending the passenger to the flight. Each & every airline operator has created their own job responsibility for ground staff.
